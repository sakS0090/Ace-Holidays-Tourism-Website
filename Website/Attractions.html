Janeshwar Mishra Park

Janeshwar Mishra Park is the pride of Lucknow. It's lush green grounds, grooves, fruit trees, water bodies with colourful fish, open gym, children's area, rubber track, war tanks and plane and immensity leaves one awestruck. Monsoons wash the greens and enhance not only beauty but freshness. Taking morning walks and exercising is a great experience here. Mornings bring alive the chirping and twittering of birds..! Indeed a gift for ucknowites. Evenings attract picnikers and fun seekers.

Ambedkar Memorial Park

Ambedkar Memorial Park is a public park and memorial in Gomti Nagar, Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, India. It is more formally known as Dr. Bhimrao Ambedkar Samajik Parivartan Prateek Sthal (Hindi: डॉ. भीमराव अम्बेडकर सामाजिक परिवर्तन प्रतीक स्थल ) and is also referred to simply as "Ambedkar Park". The park honors the lives and memories of Jyotirao Phule, Narayana Guru, Birsa Munda, Shahuji Maharaj, Bhimrao Ambedkar, Kanshi Ram and all those who've devoted their life for humanity, equality and social justice.
